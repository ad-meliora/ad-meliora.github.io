Services

Ask Us Anything!

Ask us anything about the college application process, from choosing high school courses to balancing extracurriculars to developing an application "spike."

Essay Editing

We will edit your college or scholarship essay using Google Docs and leave detailed comments and suggestions. All edits will be returned to you within four business days.

Crafting Your Narrative

We will go through your activities and interests with you and help you craft a unique narrative to share through your application that will help you stand out.

Essay Brainstorm Session

Suffering from writer's block? Need help brainstorming essay ideas? We can help you come up with and plan what to write in your essays. Note: we will not write your essays for you.

Building a College List

We can help you determine what colleges, as well as special programs fit your interests and craft a college list with adequate safeties, matches, and reaches.

Mock Interview

Worried about your interview? We can help you prepare for college alumni interviews and scholarship interviews! We will share advice on proper etiquette and how to tackle tricky questions.

School Smarts

Have a specific school in mind? We will connect you to someone from that school who can provide insider insight into the school's culture and what they look for in their applicants.

How We Work

As recent college applicants who have gained admission to highly-selective universities ourselves, we know that the college application process can be confusing and daunting at times. We want to help you make this journey a little less stressful and a lot more exciting.
